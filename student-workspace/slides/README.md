# Presentation Slides

Place your PowerPoint (.pptx) or PDF presentation file in this directory.

The executive personas and evaluation tools can reference your slides alongside your project brief and presentation notes.

## Supported Formats
- PowerPoint (.pptx)
- PDF (.pdf)

## Tips
- Keep slides visual and minimal - the notes should contain the detail
- Use the presentation-notes.md template to organize your talking points
- Practice fitting your content into the 10-minute time limit
