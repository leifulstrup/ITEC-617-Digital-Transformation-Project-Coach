# Dimension 2: Technology Selection & Feasibility

**Composite Weight: 10%**

## Description

Assesses the team's understanding of the chosen emerging technology, their rationale for
selecting it over alternatives, and whether it is a realistic fit for the organization's
technical environment. Strong submissions demonstrate that the team has moved beyond
surface-level enthusiasm to critically evaluate the technology's maturity, integration
requirements, and architectural implications.

## Related Personas

- **CTO** - Evaluates technical depth, architecture fit, and engineering feasibility
- **CIO** - Assesses enterprise integration, IT strategy alignment, and operational readiness

## Criteria

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Technology Understanding | 20% | Does the team demonstrate genuine understanding of how the technology works, not just what it does? Are core concepts accurately explained? |
| 2 | Alternatives Considered | 15% | Were other technologies or approaches evaluated? Is there a clear rationale for why this technology was chosen over alternatives? |
| 3 | Maturity Assessment / TRL | 20% | Has the team assessed the technology's maturity level (e.g., Technology Readiness Level)? Do they acknowledge limitations of emerging tech? |
| 4 | Architecture Fit | 25% | How well does the proposed technology integrate with the target organization's existing IT architecture, systems, and data flows? |
| 5 | Build/Buy/Subscribe Decision | 20% | Has the team considered whether to build a custom solution, buy a product, or subscribe to a service? Is the rationale sound? |

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Level | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Not Addressed | Topic not mentioned or considered |
| 2 | Superficial | Acknowledged but lacks depth or evidence |
| 3 | Adequate | Reasonable treatment with some supporting evidence |
| 4 | Strong | Well-developed with evidence, frameworks, and analysis |
| 5 | Exceptional | Comprehensive, nuanced, with creative insights and strong evidence |
